# Technical Bridge Formulas

### 1. Angular to Linear (Revolute Joints)
$$V_{tip} = \omega \cdot L$$
- $V_{tip}$: Velocity at the tip (m/s)
- $\omega$: Hardware Max (rad/s)
- $L$: Link Length (m)

### 2. The Perceptual Floor (Weber-Fechner Law)
$$P_{min} = X_{max} \cdot k$$
- $k_{motion} \approx 0.02$
- $k_{light} \approx 0.01$

### 3. Height Capping (Semantic Zones)
- **Eye Zone ($>1.6m$):** $0.4x$ Comfort Multiplier
- **Chest Zone ($1.2m-1.6m$):** $0.7x$ Comfort Multiplier
- **Work Zone ($0.4m-1.2m$):** $1.0x$ Comfort Multiplier
- **Ground Zone ($<0.4m$):** $1.2x$ Comfort Multiplier
